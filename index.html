Our Specialities

01

Black Pepper Chicken

Savor the robust flavors of our Black Pepper Chicken, featuring tender chicken pieces marinated in a blend of freshly ground black pepper, soy sauce, garlic, and ginger, stir-fried to perfection with bell peppers and onions. This dish offers a perfect balance of heat and savory richness, served with steamed jasmine rice.
02

Chicken Cafreal

Enjoy the aromatic and spicy taste of Chicken Cafreal, a classic Goan dish made with chicken marinated in a vibrant green paste of fresh coriander, mint, green chilies, and a blend of spices. The chicken is then pan-fried or grilled until succulent and bursting with flavor, often served with potato wedges or steamed rice.
03

Murg Musallam

Indulge in the royal flavors of Murg Musallam, a lavish Mughlai dish featuring a whole chicken marinated with a rich mixture of yogurt, spices, and herbs. The chicken is then slow-cooked to tender perfection in a creamy gravy made from onions, tomatoes, almonds, and aromatic spices like cardamom, cloves, and saffron. This grand dish is traditionally served with fragrant basmati rice or warm naan bread.
